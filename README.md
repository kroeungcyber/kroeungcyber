## About Me

I am an NGO IT Support Engineer and Digital Capability Builder with a foundational background in International Relations. My work focuses on strengthening the digital resilience of Non-Governmental Organizations operating in high-risk, resource-constrained environments across Cambodia.

I specialize in engineering **denied-by-default** security frameworks — ensuring that access to sensitive beneficiary and organizational data is never assumed, only explicitly granted through verified, policy-driven controls.
