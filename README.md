# kroeungcyber | NGO IT Support Engineer

**Bridging Technology and Humanitarian Impact through Secure Digital Ecosystems**

I am a NGO IT Support Engineer and Digital Capability Builder with a foundational background in International Relations. My work focuses on strengthening the digital resilience of Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) operating in high-risk, resource-constrained environments like Cambodia.

I specialize in engineering "denied-by-default" security frameworks—ensuring that access to sensitive beneficiary and organizational data is never assumed, only explicitly granted through verified, policy-driven controls.

---

## Key Technical Expertise

- **Security Architecture**: Implementing Zero-Trust principles via Microsoft Entra (Conditional Access, MFA) and Windows Firewall optimization to reduce organizational attack surfaces.
- **GRC & Data Governance**: Translating high-level privacy policies into automated technical controls, such as data retention/deletion triggers (e.g., purging inactive records after 300 days) and audit logging.
- **Infrastructure Management**: Deploying SD-WAN policies across sub-offices and managing network segmentation to safeguard sensitive NGO data.
- **M365 Ecosystem**: Advanced administration of SharePoint Online, Teams, and Power Automate with a focus on secure content handling and Microsoft Purview compliance.
