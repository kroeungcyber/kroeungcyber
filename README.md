## 🛠️ What I Do

### AI-Assisted Service Desk Engineering
Designing intelligent ticket intake pipelines using Azure OpenAI entity extraction and sentiment scoring — routing requests automatically while respecting regional data residency requirements.

### Automated Incident Response
Building Logic Apps and Sentinel automation runbooks that trigger remediation playbooks, deliver Adaptive Card notifications, and generate stakeholder briefings — without human bottlenecks.

### Knowledge & Field Operations Automation
Migrating legacy SOP libraries into governed vector search experiences with role-based access and multilingual support, so field teams get answers in their language, instantly.

### Digital Workplace Governance
Implementing Conditional Access policies, Purview data governance, and Defender for Cloud Apps to protect sensitive beneficiary data across distributed, high-risk environments.

### Human-Centered Change Enablement
Designing adoption frameworks and training programs that help service desk teams and field staff collaborate effectively with AI copilots — in their language, at their pace.
